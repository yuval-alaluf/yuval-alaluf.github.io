About Me

I am a PhD student studying Computer Science at Tel Aviv University, under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or. My research centers around leveraging generative models to give users greater control and creative freedom when interacting with visual content.

Recently, I joined Pika as a Research Scientist, focusing on video synthesis.

Previously, I interned at Snap Research under Kfir Aberman with a focus on personalizing generative models.

I have also had the opportunity to work as an applied researcher and tech lead. In these roles, I led diverse projects focusing on tasks such as few-shot image classification, object detection, representation learning, and uncertainty estimation.
